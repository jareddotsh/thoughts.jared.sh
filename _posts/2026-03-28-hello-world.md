This is the first post on my blog. I publish here directly from my [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md) notes.

## How it works

I write notes in Obsidian and sync them to this repository. Jekyll builds the site and GitHub Pages serves it.

Posts live in `_posts/` and follow the `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md` naming convention.
Notes (more evergreen content) live in `_notes/`.

## Writing in Obsidian

Standard markdown works out of the box. Frontmatter like this post's header is supported natively by Obsidian and Jekyll alike.

```yaml
---
layout: post
title: "My Post Title"
date: 2026-03-28
tags: [example, obsidian]
---
```
