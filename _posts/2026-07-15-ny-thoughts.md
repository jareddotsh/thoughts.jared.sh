something about this city brings out the worst and best of me…

### control

i realized im way more of a control freak then i thought, and nothing about this city is in your control, its chaos

i went with a rather large crew and I simply couldn't let go

the moments i did, it was quite peaceful

control only steals peace


### alone

it's easy to feel alone in this life, its easy to think you need to do it all by yourself. but the truth is, you can't.

this city, is a living breathing oxymoron. well i'm sure not everyone who lives here is like this. everyone is trying to become self made, well this city would not function without group effort.

finding your crew is so important, so go out there and find it. it's not easy, but necessary

### lost

seriously think we are dumber for it. but with out our phones, we would be lost.

even with them we were.

what ever happened to asking a local for for directions

and what's so wrong with being lost

we always want a life of adventure, wandering, but refused to get lost.

heads down, missing the point.

fin
